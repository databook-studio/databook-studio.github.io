FAQ

Pricing FAQ

+ What happens when the trial ends?
Read-only browsing stays available — schema explorer, notebooks, and saved queries. Running queries and SAYA AI require a license key to unlock.
+ Can I use SAYA AI with a free local model?
Yes. Ollama runs entirely on-device at no extra cost. Cloud providers (Claude, OpenAI, Gemini) are billed by their respective APIs — you bring your own key.
+ Is my data sent to the cloud?
Not unless you enable a cloud provider and turn off privacy mode. By default, schema metadata and search indexes stay local. See security & privacy.
